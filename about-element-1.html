About Us

Who we are

We are the reliable and trustworthy partner that institutions and corporations in the research and scientific community turn to in order to help their work shine



Vision

Developing into a Centre of Excellence for Equipment Knowledge Research and Integrating Basic Sciences with Engineering Applications, to Produce World Class, Industry Ready, Skilled Young Engineers in the Field of Petrochemical, Petroleum, Biotechnology, and Chemical Engineering with Capability to Developing Sustainable Processes and Systems, Yielding Innovative Products Intended to Serve the Nation and the Global Community.

Mission

➢ To develop industry-ready career-oriented internships through creative approaches to learning about and using the equipment.
➢ To equip students with the design and development skills necessary to create intelligent services and products that adhere to global standards.
➢ To encourage cooperative research and learning with business, the government, and other nations for ongoing knowledge transfer and improvement.
➢ To develop leadership, teamwork, communication, moral principles, and entrepreneurial abilities in order to benefit society.
